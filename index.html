About Me

Sobre Mim

Hi there! I'm Matheus, a passionate bi analyst and developer with over 2 years of experience building dashboards and modern web applications. After graduating with a degree in Internet Systems in 2024, I've worked as an intern at a renowned law firm where I was able to learn and be hired for a junior analyst position delivering robust and scalable solutions that solve real-world problems.

I specialize in BI area, particularly Power BI and Python, but I'm always exploring new technologies and approaches. My philosophy centers around writing clean, maintainable code and creating intuitive user experiences.

When I'm not coding, you'll find me playing some game, reading sci-fi novels, or experimenting with new cooking recipes. I believe that diverse interests fuel creativity and problem-solving in tech.
